Maxxi Museum

MAXXI Museum is known for its events and exhibitions focused on contemporary art and architecture. Maxxi showcases work from all around the world giving exposure to contemporary creativity. The exhibition we focused on was Superstudio 50. They are known for their conceptual architecture work and they focus on social change through design while keeping in mind the possibilities and limits of architecture.

Aim

Our aim for this project is to promote the events in Maxxi Museum by formation of posters and creation of a landing site which will aim on giving information regarding the events in Maxxi Museum.


Design Qualities/ Inspiration

Scale Spacing and Typography

Using a mixture of scale and spacing of typography influences the hierarchy of the texts in design. This makes the type more dynamic and gives the audience a flow to follow.


Poster Designing


Poster 1

This poster uses the space between the text in order to create more attention towards the topic.

Law of Uniform correctness is being used here for certain pictuures that give out an idea about what the poster is about.


Poster 2

Uses overlapping Images

Uses big font sizes on transparent images in order to make the text pop out


Texture

Creating textures by overlaying images and gradients of different colors and transparencies. This builds a sense of contrast in the design and enables the contrasting content to attracts the viewer.


Poster on Texture Design

Iterations

Iterations

The idea behind the first iteration was from the second poster on the mood board where I tried to use whitespace along with red color to show the importance of the event as it states "Heroic Voices". Red color resembles dominance and white text color blends in well.

For second and third iteration I used pastel color on the background that gave out more emphasis to the red color which blends in with the Civil War Theme of the poster.

For the final iteration, the texture represents civil war and the white color of the text reflects purity and helps with the content.The scale has a literal representation of the text, which creates mental connections for the viewer and utilization of different typefaces to create a difference in weight and scale in order to make a clear hierarchy
